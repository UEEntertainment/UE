About ARCA

ARCA LABELS is a musical subsidiary of UE Entertainment that sails between emotion, talent and sound. We specialize in authentic girl groups with fresh, deep concepts like the multicultural VIBE. Inspired by the ocean, we believe music can cross borders and reach the heart.
